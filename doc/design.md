# Oh-The-Dark-Path-Ahead

2D Unity game that is part platformer, part horror, and part survival.

Oh The Dark Path Ahead is going to be a 2D game, mainly a platformer though with many elements of horror and survival within it. There is going to be side to side movement, but with this prototype also being intended to test out physics, various platforming physics and things like rope and wall climbing will also be added into the movement of the character.

There will be enemies, but that is more a stretch goal for this prototype rather than a main goal. The focus being the platforming and physics of that for now. I do also want to try to make and/or grab art for this though that will also likely be a stretch goal.

The prototype will mainly go through one long linear area that would act as the tutorial for the rest of it and possibly a bit of a first level. If this were to become a full game the levels would be linear, but offer freedom to explore the world.

As requested, a basic narrative will be included for the game: you are a young creature who has spent a long time hiding within their home as instructed by its father. The father eventually had to leave into the infected world, though when they fail to return it is up to you to retrace their steps and find him.
